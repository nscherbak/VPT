What it's for. Run it after the app's code has been changed, before you bother singing into it. Green means the maths survived the edit — if something still feels wrong, it's the browser, mic, or audio, not the grading. Red means don't waste your breath; something got broken.

What it can't tell you. Whether the app actually works in your browser. Only singing into it can tell you that.
